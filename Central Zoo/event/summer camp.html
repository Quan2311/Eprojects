About Our Summer Camp

Our Summer Camp in the Zoo offers a unique opportunity for children to learn about and interact with animals in a fun and safe environment. Led by experienced and knowledgeable zoo educators, our campers will discover the world of animals through games, activities, and up-close encounters.

Our Summer Camp Programs

Wild Adventures

Join our expert zoo guides on a journey through the wilds of the zoo. Discover exotic animals and their habitats, and learn about conservation efforts to protect them.

First, we should learn about endangered species in their world to realize how important and wonderful they are. Teaching our friends about the world of wildlife has played an important role in protecting endangered animals. Second, governments around the world should open more national parks and wildlife sanctuaries. Furthermore, each country should apply more severe punishments to prevent human poaching of rare animals. In addition, we should recycle and buy sustainable products instead of buying products made from endangered animals. In short, the text “How do species live together?” gave useful information about species diversity on Earth and confirmed that all species are closely related to each other and influence each other. Therefore, we need to join hands to protect the animals on our planet.
