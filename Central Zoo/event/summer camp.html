About Our Summer Camp

Our Summer Camp in the Zoo offers a unique opportunity for children to learn about and interact with animals in a fun and safe environment. Led by experienced and knowledgeable zoo educators, our campers will discover the world of animals through games, activities, and up-close encounters.

Our Summer Camp Programs

Wild Adventures

Join our expert zoo guides on a journey through the wilds of the zoo. Discover exotic animals and their habitats, and learn about conservation efforts to protect them.

Learn More

Zoo Safari

Embark on a safari adventure as we explore the different regions of the world and their unique wildlife. Get up-close with some of our animals and learn about their adaptations.

Learn More

Animal Caretaker

Put yourself in the shoes of a zookeeper and learn how we care for our animals. From preparing food to cleaning the barn, get hands-on experience with animal care with us.
